About Me

Hi, I’m Saka Bidemi, a result-driven Data Analyst with a strong foundation in data storytelling, dashboard development, and business intelligence. I specialize in using tools like Power BI, Excel, and SQL to uncover insights, track performance, and drive business decisions across diverse sectors.
